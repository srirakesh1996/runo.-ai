Runo is a SIM-based call management and CRM solution designed to help businesses manage customer interactions more efficiently. It integrates call management with customer relationship management to provide a comprehensive platform for handling calls and customer data.

Runo works by using SIM cards to route calls through mobile devices or SIM gateways. It then integrates these calls with its CRM platform, allowing businesses to track, record, and analyze call data seamlessly.

Key features include call tracking, call recording, customer data management, real-time analytics, advanced caller ID, and integration with other business tools.

Runo improves customer service, automates workflows, reduces call management costs, and provides data-driven insights for decision-making.
